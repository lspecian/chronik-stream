Chronik Operator has been installed.

1. Check the operator is running:

   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "chronik-operator.name" . }}

2. Apply a ChronikStandalone resource:

   kubectl apply -f - <<EOF
   apiVersion: chronik.io/v1alpha1
   kind: ChronikStandalone
   metadata:
     name: my-chronik
   spec:
     image: ghcr.io/chronik-stream/chronik-server:latest
     storage:
       size: 50Gi
   EOF

3. Check its status:

   kubectl get chronikstandalones

For full documentation, visit:
  https://github.com/chronik-stream/chronik-stream/tree/main/docs
